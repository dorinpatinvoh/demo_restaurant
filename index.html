Our Story

Welcome to Savanna Spice, where tradition meets modern culinary art. Located in the heart of the city, we bring you the rich, diverse flavors of the African continent.

Our chefs use strictly fresh, locally sourced ingredients and age-old spices to create dishes that comfort the soul. Whether you are craving spicy Jollof, tender Suya, or a refreshing Hibiscus tea, we have something special for you.
